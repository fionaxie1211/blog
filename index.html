Since I was a child, I have been travel with my parents in China and paid visit to many museums in China. Contributed by my father's habbit, I have learned pretty much knowledge about Chinese relics and turn to show a great interest in populizing traditional Chinese culture through Chinese relics. However, due to the demage of war , countless valuable relics were looted and spread around the world. Nowadays, we get the chance to appreciate these relics in museums around the world. In order to provide a guide map for people who are interest in appreciating these Chinese relics around the world, I start my first map within the data in the United States. My first step after finish the map is to do a field trip by myself. After half year of busy school life in baltimore, I finally got the chance to pay a four-day visit to New York City.Metropolis Museum of Art is definately on my to go list. However, this is not only a new experience of my life in the States, but also a great step for my project. After this trip, I am officially the first user of my map.
